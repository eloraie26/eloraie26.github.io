Architecture

Three independent layers of intellectual property. Each defensible on its own; together, the moat.

CML substrate

A term-rewriting algebra from the alphabet up. Claims reduce to a canonical form; a checker confirms identity to the last bit.

CAIOS runtime

Compiles reasoning to a mmap-able binary, ships it encrypted, re-checks every certificate on open — encrypted at rest, sound on use.

Agent fleet

Specialist reasoning cells (the Constellation) that share verified facts and re-verify everything they receive.

The Constellation of Claires

One “Claire” is a single reasoning cell — generate-then-verify, with its own proof bank. Chain many and you get a federation that stays sound under composition, because every cell re-verifies each fact it receives at the boundary. A lying or faulty cell is contained, never propagated.
